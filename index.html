Boost your efficiency!

App built by Programmers for Programmers

TimeOutAI is a free powered by machine learning app which tracks your efficiency as a programmer in real time and give you a personolised advices about your working regime. Track peaks of you efficiency or when you are too low to continue.

Based on your personal schedule smart alghoritms will give you a recommendations how you can drastically boost your productivity and become a superhero-programmer! Try it now and see the difference!
